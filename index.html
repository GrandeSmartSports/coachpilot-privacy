CoachPilot - Practice Plans
Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 19, 2026

CoachPilot - Practice Plans ("the App") is developed by Daniel Grande. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

In short: The App does not collect personal data, does not require an account, does not track you, and does not use analytics. Your data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required to use the App.
  • No personal data (name, email, location, or device identifiers) is collected.
  • No analytics or usage tracking of any kind is used.
  • No cookies or similar tracking technologies are used.

2. How We Use Information

On the free tier, practice plans are generated entirely on your device using a built-in drill database. No information leaves your device during this process.

On the Pro tier, if you choose to use AI-powered plan generation, roster data you provide (such as player names and skill levels) is sent to the Claude AI API to generate personalized practice plans. This data is used solely for the purpose of generating your plan and is not stored by the API after the response is returned. No other data is sent.

3. Data Storage

All practice plans, roster information, and app settings are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your data. If you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed.

4. Third-Party Services

The free tier of the App does not use any third-party services.

The Pro tier uses the Claude AI API (provided by Anthropic) to generate AI-powered practice plans. When you use this feature, roster data is transmitted to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by their privacy policy. The data sent is not stored after your plan is generated.

No other data is shared with any third parties.

5. Camera and Photo Library

The App may request access to your device's camera or photo library to enable roster scanning via OCR (optical character recognition). This feature uses Apple's on-device Vision framework to read text from images.

  • All image processing occurs entirely on your device.
  • Images are never uploaded to any server.
  • The App does not store the original images after processing.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Because the App does not collect any personal data from any user, it does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
